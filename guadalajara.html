Common questions

Is Guadalajara safe for tourists?

Guadalajara requires heightened awareness for tourists. Opportunistic theft is the most common risk. Stick to established tourist areas, use registered taxis or ride-hailing apps, and avoid unfamiliar areas at night.

Is Guadalajara safe at night?

Night-time safety in Guadalajara varies significantly by neighbourhood. Stick to established areas, use ride-hailing apps rather than hailing taxis on the street, and avoid unfamiliar areas after dark.
