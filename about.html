Sources & Coverage

96 cities · 21 countries · every rating traced to a named source

  • Ratings are built from official government advisories (US State Dept, UK FCDO, Canada DFATD, Australia Smartraveller) plus official crime statistics where a direct API exists — never from world-knowledge alone.
  • Direct stat APIs wired: SSP-SP (São Paulo state), ISP-RJ (Rio), SNIC (Argentina — 5 cities), MinDefensa (Colombia — 7 cities), PDI Chile (Santiago, Valparaíso, Concepción), Uruguay OSP (Montevideo). World Bank / UNODC homicide rates cover all remaining countries.
  • All remaining cities use the World Bank / UNODC national homicide indicator (VC.IHR.PSRC.P5) as the crime-data baseline — explicitly noted as country-level, not city-specific.
  • Every source link below is live and fetchable — no editorial stubs, no fabricated numbers.
