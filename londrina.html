What the sources say

Londrina scores Moderate. This major Paraná state agricultural city has a developed economy and moderate crime profile — elevated by global standards but manageable within Brazil.

Common questions

Is Londrina safe for tourists?

Londrina is safe for tourists who exercise reasonable vigilance. Stay in well-visited areas, avoid displaying expensive electronics or jewellery, and be cautious after dark in less-familiar neighbourhoods.

Is Londrina safe at night?

Londrina is reasonably safe at night in well-known tourist and residential areas. Avoid displaying valuables, use reputable transport, and be cautious in unfamiliar neighbourhoods after dark.

What are the main safety risks for visitors to Londrina?

The primary risks for visitors to Londrina are opportunistic theft, phone snatching, and pickpocketing in crowded areas. Brazil authorities note crime, particularly in urban areas.
